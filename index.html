FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is KoreFlow free to use?

Yes. KoreFlow is free to download and use. You only pay when you actually book a service through a partner provider.

How accurate are the estimates?

Estimates combine your inputs (rooms, items, distance, service tier) with regional pricing data. They are designed to be within 5–10% of a final quote.

Do you collect my photos?

Photos you attach stay on your device. We do not upload them to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Where is my data stored?

Saved quotes and your profile live locally on your device. You can clear them at any time from Settings.

Can I edit an estimate after saving?

Yes. Open any saved quote from the History tab and adjust inputs to refresh the breakdown instantly.
