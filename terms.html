Quick troubleshooting

  • Estimates look off? Verify room counts, item counts, and the pickup/drop-off distance in Settings.
  • Photos won't attach? Check iOS Settings → Privacy → Photos → KoreFlow.
  • Location not detected? Allow "While Using the App" in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → KoreFlow.
  • Lost a saved quote? If you reinstalled the App, local data may not have been restored from iCloud. Reach out to Amirjadoon8811@gmail.com and we will do our best to help.
