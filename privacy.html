1. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the KoreFlow mobile application (the "App") provided by KoreFlow Inc. ("KoreFlow", "we", "us"). It describes the information we collect when you use the App, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By installing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information we collect

KoreFlow is designed to keep your project data on your device. We do not collect personal information from you, and we do not transmit the data you enter to our servers.

Information stored locally on your device

  • Project inputs: rooms, item counts, addresses, photos, and notes you attach to an estimate.
  • Saved quotes: a history of estimates you choose to archive locally.

The App does not include analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs, and it does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or device identifiers for advertising purposes. The only data KoreFlow reads from your device is the information you provide directly.

3. How we use your information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Generate, save, and display your renovation and moving estimates.
  • Calculate distance, taxes, and service-tier pricing based on your inputs.
  • Provide customer support when you contact us.
  • Diagnose crashes, prevent abuse, and improve the App.

We do not use your project data to train advertising models or to build third-party profiles.

4. Device permissions we request

KoreFlow only asks for the following iOS permissions, and only when you tap the relevant feature:

  • Photo Library (read): to attach reference photos of rooms, furniture, or fragile items to an estimate.
  • Photo Library (write): to save your finalized quote summary as an image so you can share it.
  • Camera: to capture reference photos of items to be moved or renovated.
  • Location (while in use): to calculate pickup and delivery distance and refine your estimate.
  • Contacts: to import a saved contact as your moving or renovation address details.

You can revoke any permission at any time from iOS Settings → Privacy.

5. Where your data is stored

Profile data, saved quotes, and attached photos are stored locally on your device inside the App's sandboxed storage. We do not transmit your project data to our servers.

If you enable iCloud backup on your device, items stored in the App may be included in your encrypted iCloud backup. This is governed by Apple's iCloud terms, not ours.

6. When we share information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics vendors.

  • With your action: when you tap "Share" to export a quote image via the iOS share sheet, the destination app receives only what you choose to send.
  • Legal: if required by valid law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of KoreFlow or others.
  • Business transfers: in the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice to you.

7. Your choices and controls

  • Edit profile: change your name, email, or avatar at any time in Settings → Profile.
  • Delete a quote: swipe-to-delete from the History tab.
  • Reset all data: Profile → "Clear All Saved Quotes" permanently removes every saved quote and its attached photos from your device.
  • Revoke permissions: iOS Settings → Privacy → KoreFlow.
  • Request export or deletion: email Amirjadoon8811@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's privacy

KoreFlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact Amirjadoon8811@gmail.com so we can delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. For material changes, we will notify you inside the App before they take effect.
